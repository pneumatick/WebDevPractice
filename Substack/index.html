Pretense

Nobody Recognizes My Supreme Intelligence

Aug 30

I have been telling people for the past few years that I am the biggest, most smartest, most epicest smart guy the world has ever seen, with all the best ideas ever. However, despite my efforts, everyone thinks I'm some sort of pretentious loser. What they don't know is that my mom cooks dinosaur chicken tendies every night because of how much of a special boy I am. This is clear evidence that I'm epic awesomesauce, which means that you're just a bunch of stupid haters!
